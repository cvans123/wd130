About

Hotel Booking is fully digitized from booking to departure. No paperwork. No tedious signing in at reception. No access cards or keys to lose. All functions are performed on our Salto system giving you access to front gate, front door and amenities including your bedroom from the app. Check out on your phone and pay by phone. All rooms have their own generous breakfast balcony, extra length king size bed with 300 thread count Egyptian cotton linen and 3 types of pillows to choose from the pillow menu. The extra size flat screen TV (50") is in keeping with the generously sized bedrooms and bathrooms. The suits range from 35 to 90 square meters. It goes without saying that each room has an air conditioner and a workstation for the notebook workaholic and a hairdryer station. There is a conveniently placed bedside USB port for phone charging. Low lighting LED'S that will direct you to the bathroom without having to disturb your partner. For the romantic at heart, you can light up the ceiling with star lights above the bed. The bathroom with twin vanity stone slab, separate walk-in shower, quiet toilet system and free-standing ball and claw bath. Each bathroom comes with an electric shaving plug and a state-of-the-art sliding door with a full-length mirror. All these amenities are finished off with the most luxurious soft carpet.
